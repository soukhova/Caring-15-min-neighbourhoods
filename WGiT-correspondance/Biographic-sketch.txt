Anastasia Soukhov (B.Eng, MASc.) is a PhD Candidate in Transport Geography at the School of Earth, Environment and Society at McMaster University. Her doctoral work focuses on sustainable and equitable transportation systems, namely on spatial and reproducible methods including accessibility indicators, scenario planning and spatial statistics. She is actively grounding her quantitative work on conceptualisations of gender, justice and sustainability. She is working on translating methods to practice as a researcher on the Data-Driven Equity Standards group, part of the Mobilizing Justice Partnership; a multi-sectoral partnership addressing transportation inequities in Canadian cities through research and knowledge mobilization.
